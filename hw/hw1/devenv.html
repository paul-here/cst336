Intro Basic Syntax Dev Environments Resources

A good PHP IDE can increase productivity. Because PHP is also embedded in HTML, the environment should also support HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Here are a few IDEs I would like to try:

  • Atom with Nuclide
    • Atom is highly adaptable text editor, and is open source
    • The Nuclide module is also open source, and adds PHP IDE funtionality.
  • Cloud 9
    • The greatest advantage of Cloud9 is the fact that it is fully on the cloud
    • All settings are saved between sessions -- just jump back in!
  • PHPStorm
    • Jet Brains developes very fast and thorough toolkits
    • With a .edu email address, you can get a year for free
